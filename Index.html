Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

To provide the best experience in UE5 Dev Hub, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and display name.
  • User Content: Guides, tutorials, and descriptions created by you, including any images uploaded to our platform.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with the app, such as views, likes, and navigation patterns.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the UE5 Dev Hub services.
  • To manage your account and identify your contributions within the community.
  • To allow other users to view the Unreal Engine guides you publish.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform). This includes:

  • Authentication & Database: Managed by Firebase Auth and Firestore.
  • Media Storage: Images are stored in Firebase Storage with industry-standard encryption.

4. Your Rights and Data Deletion

We respect your privacy rights. You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or correct your personal information at any time.
  • Delete your account: You can request account deletion directly within the app settings or via our official support channels. Upon deletion, all your personal data and created guides will be permanently removed from our servers.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us at:

Email: support@ue5devhub.com

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy within the app.
