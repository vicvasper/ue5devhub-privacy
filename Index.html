Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Welcome to UE5 Dev Hub. Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we handle your information within our application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a collaborative experience for Unreal Engine developers, we collect:

  • Account Data: Email address, username, and display name provided during registration.
  • User-Generated Content: Guides, tutorials, and any images you upload to the platform.
  • Interaction Data: View counts, likes, and saved favorites to personalize your feed.

2. Use of Information

We use the collected data solely to:

  • Maintain and provide the core services of the app.
  • Attribute credit to creators of guides and tutorials.
  • Improve the technical performance and content relevance of the hub.

3. Data Storage (Firebase)

We use Firebase (Google Cloud) for all our backend services. Your data is stored in secure cloud environments managed by Google, including:

  • Cloud Firestore: For text-based data and user profiles.
  • Firebase Storage: For hosting images attached to your Unreal guides.

4. Your Rights

In accordance with data protection regulations, you have the right to access, rectify, or request the deletion of your personal data at any time through the application settings or by contacting us directly.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@ue5devhub.com

6. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this policy. Any significant updates will be notified to users through an in-app announcement.
