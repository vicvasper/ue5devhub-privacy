Account Deletion

Last Updated: January 12, 2026

At UE5 Dev Hub, we respect your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request the deletion of your account and what happens to your data when you do.

How to Delete Your Account

You can request account deletion through two methods:

Option 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open UE5 Dev Hub on your device.
  2. Go to your Profile tab.
  3. Tap on Settings (gear icon).
  4. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account".
  5. Confirm your decision when prompted.

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to vic.rivasperez@gmail.com with the following information:

Subject: Account Deletion Request - UE5 Dev Hub

Body:
- Registered email address: [your email]
- Username: [your username]
- Reason for deletion (optional): [your reason]

We will process your request within 7 business days and send you a confirmation email once completed.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Profile Information: Your username, email, display name, and profile picture.
  • Account Settings: All your preferences and configurations.
  • Favorites: Your list of saved guides.
  • Following Data: Your list of followed creators.
  • Authentication Data: Your login credentials and session information.

Your Created Content

For guides and tutorials you have created, you have two options:

  • Delete All Content: All your guides will be permanently deleted along with your account.
  • Anonymize Content: Your guides can remain on the platform but will be attributed to "Deleted User" instead of your username.

Please specify your preference when requesting deletion. If no preference is provided, all content will be deleted by default.

Important Warning

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once your account is deleted, we cannot recover any of your data, guides, or purchase history. If you have made any in-app purchases, please note that these cannot be refunded or transferred after account deletion.

Data Retention

After account deletion, some information may be retained for a limited period:

  • Transaction Records: Purchase history may be retained for up to 7 years for tax and legal compliance purposes.
  • Legal Requirements: Any data required to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements.
  • Anonymized Analytics: Aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics may be retained for service improvement.

Processing Time

  • In-App Deletion: Immediate (within 24 hours)
  • Email Request: Up to 7 business days

You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been successfully deleted.
